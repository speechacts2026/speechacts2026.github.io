Description

The Speech Acts Workshop 2026 brings together researchers and students in pragmatics, semantics, and the philosophy of language to discuss recent advances in the theory and analysis of speech acts. Topics include performative verbs, indirect speech acts, contextual meaning, and interface with syntax.

This workshop is intended for linguists, philosophers, and cognitive scientists interested in the theory, empirical data, and applications of speech act theory. Participants will have the opportunity to engage in talks, discussions, and collaborative sessions.
